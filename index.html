About

Rooted in industry.
Building for tomorrow.

Saajith Juvva is a second-generation entrepreneur from Hyderabad with 24+ years of family experience in Iron and Steel castings. That legacy is the bedrock on which he has built two distinct, high-impact businesses.

As Director at Saaketh Iron Industries Pvt. Ltd., Saajith has spent the past 5+ years running day-to-day operations of one of South India's leading foundries — managing a sophisticated manufacturing facility producing 12,000 tonnes of precision castings annually for clients across textiles, automotive, hydraulics, and general engineering.

As Director & Co-Founder of Scell Energy Private Limited, he is spearheading India's first dedicated cell housing manufacturing facility — a bold bet on domestic battery manufacturing at a pivotal moment in India's energy story.

Saajith's unique strength lies in bringing deep operational knowledge from traditional manufacturing into frontier-technology ventures — bridging the old economy and the new with credibility and conviction.

Milestone

Scell Energy — Groundbreaking Ceremony

A landmark moment for India's clean energy future — the Scell Energy groundbreaking ceremony was graced by Union Cabinet Minister Shri Ashwini Vaishnaw (Minister of Railways, Information & Broadcasting, and Electronics & IT, Government of India) and Shri D. Sridhar Babu (Minister of IT, Electronics & Communications, Government of Telangana). Their presence underscored the national importance of India's first dedicated cell housing manufacturing facility and its alignment with India's vision for a self-reliant domestic battery ecosystem.

India's First Cell Housing Facility

The ceremony marked the official commencement of India's first dedicated cell can and cell cap manufacturing facility — a historic step toward a self-reliant domestic battery supply chain aligned with India's 500 GWh vision for 2035.
